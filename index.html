International Tourism Councils is a division of BeDynamic, Inc. - a Seattle based technology enterprise.

International Tourism Councils (ITC) works with destinations committed to increasing their share of global tourism revenues. ITC collaborates with Public Sector tourism organizations to develop and manage partnerships with Private Sector enterprises. ITC leverages budgets and delivers strategic cooperative marketing programs supported by technological innovation. ITC increases arrivals from target markets and enhances per capita spending by all visitors.

Tourist-Driven Increased Revenues

Direct tourism spending by visitors immediately increases public and private sector revenues. The most significant impact is on small to medium sized businesses and the subsequent creation of tourism related jobs.

Attract Foreign Trade and Investment

Efforts to attract more foreign trade and investment will be significantly enhanced by an energetic and complimentary public/private sector funded marketing campaign to promote a country’s international tourism.

Direct Spending Revenue Benefits

Every 1% increase in arrivals and/or per capita spending of all visitors corresponds to millions in USD increase in direct tourism spending along with a corresponding increase in associated public sector fees and taxes.

Indirect Spending Revenue Benefits

As revenues from direct spending on tourism flow through the larger economy the multiplier principle produces substantial increases to indirect revenues that drive new employment across all sectors of the economy.

None of Us is As Smart As All of Us

Collaboration between public organization, private enterprise, trade groups and technology combines diverse skills, experience and perspective that produce better ideas and create significant competitive advantages.

The Advantages of Cooperative Marketing

Combining strategically selected partners from the public and the private sectors will collectively leverage funding, clout, and marketing economies of scale no one partner could achieve individually.

The Power of Connection

Our online platform allows ITC partners to deliver up-to-the-minute activity and event information directly to mobile devices and PCs, empowering travelers to engage in transactions – before and after arrival.

The Data Advantage

Our proprietary platform collects, as well as delivers data. User activity is documented, analyzed, stored and used to maximize marketing efforts directed to travelers with a demonstrated interest in your destination.

Experience Matters

The ITC team is rich with the necessary marketing and management experience but that experience means nothing without the dynamic and enthusiastic commitment and collaboration of its public/private sector partners.
